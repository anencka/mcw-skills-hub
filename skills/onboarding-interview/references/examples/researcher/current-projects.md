# Current Projects

### Home gait monitoring for Parkinson's
- **What it is:** A wearable + pipeline that quantifies gait at home and flags clinically meaningful change.
- **Status:** In progress — validation study enrolling (mid-way).
- **My role:** PI; I own the study design and the analysis pipeline.
- **Key collaborators:** Dr. Singh (Neurology, clinical lead); James, postdoc (signal processing).
- **Funding / sponsor:** NIH R01 (active, year 2 of 5; progress report due Q3).
- **What "done" looks like:** A published validation showing our metric tracks clinician ratings, plus a locked pipeline.
- **Priority right now:** High.
