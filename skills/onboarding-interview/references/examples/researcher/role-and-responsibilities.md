# Role & Responsibilities

## What I'm accountable for
The lab's research direction and funding, my trainees' progress and well-being, IRB compliance for
our studies, and delivering on the milestones in our active grants. If a grant report is late or a
study drifts out of protocol, that's on me.

## Regular cadences
- **Weekly:** lab meeting (Mon), 1:1s with each trainee (rolling), clinical-collaborator sync (Thu).
- **Monthly:** data-review with the Neurology co-I; departmental faculty meeting.
- **Quarterly:** grant progress reports when due; conference-deadline crunches; budget review with
  the grants administrator.

## Routine decisions I make
Which experiments go next, whether a participant's data is usable, how to triage a trainee's
competing priorities, what to cut when a paper is over length, whether a result is solid enough to
present.

## What I produce
Manuscripts, grant proposals and progress reports, IRB protocols and amendments, analysis code and
pipelines, conference talks and posters, and trainee feedback (drafts, committee letters).

## Reporting
- I report to: the BME Department Chair (and program officers on each grant).
- Reports to me: 2 postdocs, 3 PhD students, 1 staff engineer.
